Sustainable Development Goal 11: Sustainable Cities and Communities

Subgoals

11.1 – "Adequate, safe, and affordable housing and basic services"
11.2 – "Safe, affordable, and accessible transport systems"
11.3 – "Enhance inclusive and sustainable urbanization"
11.4 – "Safeguard the world’s cultural and natural heritage"
11.5 – "Reduce the number of deaths and economic losses caused by disasters"
11.6 – "Reduce the adverse per capita environmental impact of cities"
11.7 – "Provide universal access to accessible, green and public spaces"

What is SDG 11?

Sustainable Development Goal 11, "Sustainable Cities and Communities" , concerns itself with urban living and how its environmental impacts can be mitigated, along with how it can be improved in general.

Suggested ways of achieving these goals:

- 11.a – "Support positive economic, social and environmental links between urban and rural areas"

- 11.b – "Increase the number of cities and human settlements adopting and implementing integrated policies and plans towards inclusion, resource efficiency, etc."
